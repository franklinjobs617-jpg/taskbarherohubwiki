## Sorcerer: The Glass Cannon

Sorcerer has the highest damage ceiling in TBH — **1650% base crit damage** and **50% base crit chance**. In exchange, you get the lowest survivability: 50 HP and 5 armor. One hit from a Torment enemy will kill you.

This is not a beginner class. Play Sorcerer only when you understand enemy patterns and stage layouts.

### Early Game (Normal)

**Core stats:** Critical Chance > Cast Speed > Critical Damage > Attack Damage

Your 50% base crit makes early stages deceptively easy. Many enemies die in one or two hits:

- **Fireball** — Primary clear. Good AoE for early stages.
- **Arcane Blast** — Single-target finisher.
- **Teleport** — Mandatory. Bind this to an easy key. Use it preemptively, not reactively.

**Key tip:** The moment you see an enemy start moving toward you, Teleport. If you wait until they're in range, it's too late.

**Recommended stage:** Normal 1-1. Sorcerer needs levels before attempting harder content.

### Mid Game (Nightmare)

**Core stats:** Critical Chance (65%+) > Critical Damage > Cast Speed > Attack Damage

Pivot to crit damage after hitting 65% crit rate:

- **Arcane Nova** — Replaces Fireball as your main clear. Larger AoE, more damage.
- **Firestorm** — Your AoE burst. Use on dense packs or when enemies group up.
- **Teleport + positioning** — Master the art of kiting. Never stand still for more than 2 seconds.

**Recommended stage:** Nightmare 1-3. High monster density but predictable spawns.

### End Game (Hell / Torment)

**Core stats:** Critical Damage > Critical Chance > Cast Speed > Max HP

At end game, push crit damage beyond 2000%:

- **Rune priority:** AllHeroCriticalDamage > AllHeroCriticalChance > AdditionalGold
- One Arcane Nova + Firestorm rotation should delete most of a Torment enemy's HP bar.
- Consider adding Mana Shield for Hell/Torment boss encounters.

**Stage strategy:** Sorcerer performs best on stages where enemies group up and approach predictably. Avoid stages with spread-out enemies or ranged attackers.

### Rune Priority

1. **AllHeroCriticalChance (must)** — Push to 65%+ first.
2. **AllHeroCriticalDamage (must)** — Scale 1650% base to 2000%+.
3. **AdditionalGold (optional)** — Extra value from high-efficiency stages.
4. **AllHeroMaxHp (optional)** — Only if you need the survivability buffer.

### The Golden Rule of Sorcerer

> If you get hit, you made a positioning mistake, not a gear problem.

Sorcerer's survivability is not measured in HP or armor. It's measured in how well you avoid damage. Teleport timing, kiting distance, and enemy pattern knowledge are more important than any stat on your gear.
