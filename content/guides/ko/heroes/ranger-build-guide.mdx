## Why Ranger Dominates Farming

Ranger is the fastest farming class in TBH because of one number: **100 base attack speed**. No other class comes close. Every other stat you build should support this core advantage.

The Ranger philosophy is simple: **hit more, not harder.** A Ranger that fires 3 times for every 1 Knight swing will out-farm the Knight in most content, even with lower per-hit damage.

### Early Game (Normal Stages)

**Core stats:** Attack Speed > Movement Speed > Attack Damage > Critical Chance

Your early priority is to push Attack Speed as high as possible. Use the following skills:

- **Multi Shot** — Your primary clear. Hits multiple targets and clears packs faster than single-target alternatives.
- **Quick Draw** — Temporary ASPD buff. Keep this active during clears.
- **Evasion** — Use offensively. The attack cycle reset after Evasion lets you chain Multi Shots faster.

**Gear direction:** Prioritize weapons and armor with Attack Speed and Movement Speed substats. The base Ranger bow gives you a good start — upgrade to anything with +ASPD first, worry about other stats later.

**Recommended stage:** Normal 1-3 — fast clear, spread-out enemies, good early drops.

### Mid Game (Nightmare)

**Core stats:** Attack Speed (180+) > Critical Chance (50%+) > Critical Damage > Attack Damage

This is where Ranger's power curve changes. Once your Attack Speed hits 180+, the marginal return on more speed decreases. Begin pivoting to Critical stats:

- **Critical Eye** — Essential. Every other shot crits at 50%+ crit rate. This is your biggest damage multiplier.
- **Multi Shot + Critical Eye combo** — Each Multi Shot projectile can crit independently. With 6 targets and 50% crit rate, you average 3 crits per cast.
- **Evasion** — Still essential for attack reset.

**Recommended stage:** Nightmare 1-3 — highest monster density per clear, maximizes your clear-speed advantage.

### End Game (Hell / Torment)

**Core stats:** Critical Damage > Critical Chance > Attack Speed > Attack Damage

At end game, your Attack Speed should be 200+ without trying. Max out Critical Damage:

- The difference between 50% crit rate / 150% crit damage vs 50% rate / 250% damage is enormous.
- Push Critical Damage through runes and high-level gear.
- Consider the `AllHeroCriticalDamage` rune as your first priority.

**Ranger vs Boss:** Ranger is not designed for boss content. At end game, Ranger can handle Hell bosses with excellent gear, but Torment bosses require a dedicated boss-killer class. Use the Farming Optimizer to find stages where Ranger's speed advantage matters most.

### Rune Priority

1. **AllHeroAttackSpeed (must)** — Non-negotiable. This is your core stat.
2. **AllHeroCriticalChance (must)** — Pivot to this after 180 ASPD.
3. **AllHeroMovementSpeed (must)** — Clear speed depends on stage traversal.
4. **AllHeroCriticalDamage (must)** — Endgame scaling.
5. **AdditionalExp (optional)** — Helpful during leveling.

### Common Mistakes

- Building Attack Damage before Attack Speed. Speed first, always.
- Standing still. Ranger's survival is mobility, not armor. Keep moving.
- Using boss stages for farming. Ranger clears normal stages 2-3x faster than boss stages per unit of loot.
- Not using the Farming Compare tool to validate stage efficiency with your actual clear times.
