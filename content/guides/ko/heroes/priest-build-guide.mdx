## Priest: The Unkillable Class

Priest is the safest class in TBH with **95 HP and 30 armor** — the best balanced survivability in the game. A Priest with good HP Regen is effectively immortal against normal stage enemies.

### Sustain Build (Early Game — Idle Specialist)

**Core stats:** HP Regen > Max HP > Armor > Cooldown

The sustain build is the easiest way to play TBH. Your goal is to reach a point where you can start a stage, walk away, and come back to loot:

- **Heal** — Core ability. Keep it active at all times. The uptime on Heal determines how long you can idle.
- **Holy Shield** — Emergency damage reduction. Use when Heal is on cooldown and taking damage.
- **Smite** — Your only damage source in this build. It's not impressive but it's enough.

**HP Regen target:** Aim for enough HP Regen to outpace the incoming damage from your chosen stage. If you dip below 80% HP during idle, you need more regen.

**Recommended stage:** Normal 1-4 or Nightmare 1-4 — safe, consistent, predictable.

### Support Build (Mid Game — Team Anchor)

**Core stats:** HP Regen > Cooldown > Max HP > Armor

In multi-hero content, Priest shifts from solo sustain to team support:

- **Blessing** — Team-wide damage and stat buff. This significantly increases your party's kill speed.
- **Heal** — Still essential. You keep everyone alive while Blessing boosts their damage.
- **Holy Shield** — Use on your squishy teammates (Sorcerer, Hunter) when they're about to take big hits.

**When to use:** Any multi-hero content, co-op farming, or when pushing progression with a balanced team.

### Farming Build (Mid/Late Game — Efficiency)

**Core stats:** Attack Damage > HP Regen > Max HP > Attack Speed

Once your sustain is solid, switch to a more aggressive approach:

- **Holy Nova** — AoE clear that out-damages Smite on packed stages.
- **Smite** — Single-target finisher.
- **Heal** — Keep this. You are not sacrificing survival — you are optimizing clear speed within your survival buffer.

**Key insight:** If you are not dying in your current stage, you have room to swap defensive gear for offensive gear. Push this edge gradually.

### Rune Priority

1. **AllHeroHpRegen (must)** — Non-negotiable. This is what makes Priest work.
2. **AllHeroMaxHp (must)** — Bigger pool = more room to idle.
3. **AllHeroArmor (must)** — Damage reduction compounds with regen.
4. **AllHeroAttackDamage (must)** — For farming build.
5. **AdditionalExp (optional)** — Level faster during idle.

### Why Play Priest?

Priest is not the fastest or the flashiest class in TBH. It doesn't have Sorcerer's crit numbers or Ranger's speed. What Priest has is the most forgiving learning curve and the safest progression path. Every other class will eventually outperform Priest at their specialty, but no class is better at keeping you alive while you learn the game.
